## Overview

Branding allows you to customize how your emails and letters appear to recipients. You can add logos, choose colors, and create a consistent visual identity for your service communications.

## Email Branding

Email branding controls the logo, colors, and header that appear in your emails.

### Choosing Email Branding

Access email branding at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding`.

#### Available Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="GOV.UK Branding">
    **Default option** - Uses the GOV.UK logo and standard government styling.
    
    **Appearance:**
    - GOV.UK logo in header
    - Black text on white background
    - Standard link colors
    - No custom branding
    
    **When to use:**
    - Default for all new services
    - Cross-government communications
    - When no specific branding needed
    
    Select at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/govuk`.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="NHS Branding">
    **NHS logo and colors** - For NHS organizations only.
    
    **Appearance:**
    - NHS logo
    - NHS blue header
    - Accessible color scheme
    
    **Eligibility:**
    Only available if your service organization type is NHS or NHS GP.
    
    Select at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/nhs`.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Organization Branding">
    **Your organization's branding** from the organization's branding pool.
    
    **Availability:**
    - Shown if your organization has created brandings
    - Multiple options may be available
    - Shared across services in your organization
    
    Preview before applying to see how it looks.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Custom Branding">
    **Create new branding** with your own logo and colors.
    
    Available for:
    - Central government (more options)
    - Local government  
    - NHS organizations
    - Other public sector bodies
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Creating Custom Email Branding

#### For Central Government

Central government services have three logo options:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Logo Type">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/choose-logo`:
    
    **Organization logo** - Your department or agency logo
    - Upload your logo
    - Add optional banner color
    
    **Government identity logo** - Single Identity System crest or insignia  
    - Request creation through support
    - Follows government branding guidelines
    - Professionally designed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Banner Style">
    For organization logos, choose banner type at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/add-banner`:
    
    **Organization logo** - Logo only in header
    - Clean, minimal appearance
    - Logo on white background
    
    **Colored banner with logo** - Logo on colored background
    - Brand color across header
    - More prominent visual identity
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Banner Color (if applicable)">
    Select banner color at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/choose-banner-colour`:
    
    **Government Identity System colors:**
    - Department for Education - Blue `#347CA9`
    - Home Office - Purple `#9325B2`
    - Ministry of Justice - Blue `#231F20`
    - And many more...
    
    **Custom color:**
    - Enter hex code (e.g., `#FF0000`)
    - Preview shows result
    - Must meet accessibility contrast requirements
    
    <Warning>
      Ensure sufficient contrast between banner color and logo for accessibility.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Logo">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/upload-logo`:
    
    **File requirements:**
    - PNG or SVG format  
    - Max file size: 2MB
    - Recommended: Transparent background
    - Minimum width: 200px
    - For SVG: No embedded text or images
    
    **Upload process:**
    - Click "Choose file" or drag and drop
    - File is validated for format and safety
    - Temporary preview generated
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Alt Text">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/preview`:
    
    Enter alternative text for screen readers:
    - Describe the logo: "Department for Transport"
    - Keep it concise
    - Don't include "logo" (already implied)
    
    **Preview shows:**
    - How email header looks
    - Logo placement
    - Color scheme
    - Example message content
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Branding">
    Confirm to save the branding:
    - Applied to your service
    - Added to organization's branding pool
    - Available for other services in your org
    - Logo stored permanently
    
    <Tip>
      If this is your organization's first branding and you have no other live services, it may become the organization default.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### For Other Organizations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Banner Type">
    Non-central government services start at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/add-banner`:
    
    Select:
    - **Organization logo** - Logo only
    - **Colored banner with logo** - Logo with background color
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Logo">
    Same requirements as central government:
    - PNG or SVG
    - Max 2MB  
    - Transparent background recommended
    - No embedded content in SVG
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Alt Text and Save">
    Enter alt text, preview, and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Requesting Government Identity Logos

For official government crests and insignia:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Single Identity Option">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/choose-logo`, select "Government single identity crest or insignia".
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Information">
    See requirements at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/request-government-identity-logo`:
    - Available crests shown
    - Usage guidelines
    - Example branding
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Logo Text">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/request-government-identity-logo/enter-text`:
    
    Provide the text for your crest:
    - Department name
    - Agency name
    - Exact wording required
    
    Example: "Department for Education"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Request">
    A support ticket is created automatically:
    - Notify team reviews request
    - Branding created professionally
    - Response within 1 working day
    - Applied to your service when ready
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Requesting Custom Branding Help

If you need assistance:

1. Go to `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/email-branding/request`
2. Describe your branding needs:
   - Logo requirements
   - Color preferences
   - Special considerations
3. Submit request
4. Notify team responds by next working day

<Note>
  Support requests create Zendesk tickets tagged with `notify_task_email_branding` or `notify_task_email_branding_gov`.
</Note>

## Letter Branding

Letter branding adds your logo to printed letters.

### Choosing Letter Branding

Access at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/letter-branding`.

#### Available Options

**No logo** - Plain letters without branding
- Default for new services
- Simple, unbranded appearance
- Text only

**NHS branding** - NHS logo on letters
- NHS organizations only  
- Standard NHS visual identity

**Organization branding** - From your organization's pool
- Pre-created brandings
- Shared across services

**Custom branding** - Upload your own logo

### Creating Letter Branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Logo">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/letter-branding/upload-branding`:
    
    **File requirements:**
    - SVG format only
    - Max 2MB
    - Black or single color
    - No text in SVG (use shapes only)
    - No embedded images
    
    **Design guidelines:**
    - Height: ~25mm recommended
    - Width: ~100mm maximum
    - Clean, simple design
    - High contrast for printing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Branding Name">
    At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/letter-branding/set-name`:
    
    Enter name for the branding:
    - Internal reference only
    - Not shown to recipients
    - Must be unique
    
    Example: "Department for Transport logo"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Save">
    Preview shows:
    - Logo placement on letter
    - Size and positioning
    - How it looks with letter content
    
    Save to:
    - Apply to your service
    - Add to organization pool
    - Make available for other services
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Letter Branding Placement

Logos appear:
- **Top right** of first page
- Above letter content
- Consistent positioning
- Printed in black (or spot color if supported)

### Requesting Letter Branding Help

1. Navigate to `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/letter-branding/request`
2. Describe requirements
3. Attach logo files if available
4. Submit for support team review

## Managing Branding

### Viewing Current Branding

Check current branding on service settings page:
- Email branding name
- Letter branding name  
- Preview links
- Change options

### Changing Branding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Branding Settings">
    From service settings, click on the branding type you want to change.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select New Branding">
    Choose from available options:
    - Organization pool
    - GOV.UK/NHS standard
    - Create new custom branding
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview Changes">
    View preview at `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/{type}-branding/confirm-change`:
    - See example message
    - Check logo appears correctly
    - Verify colors and layout
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Update">
    Click "Save" to apply:
    - Takes effect immediately
    - Applies to all future messages
    - Existing sent messages unchanged
    
    <Note>
      Test by sending yourself a message after changing branding.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Organization Branding Pools

Organizations maintain pools of approved brandings:

**Adding to pool:**
- Automatically added when you create custom branding
- Platform admins can add when setting service branding
- Shared across all services in organization

**Benefits:**
- Consistent branding across services
- No need to recreate for each service
- Centralized brand management
- Faster setup for new services

## Platform Admin Features

Platform administrators have additional branding capabilities:

### Setting Any Branding

At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/set-{type}-branding`:
- Search all brandings system-wide
- Apply any branding to any service
- Preview before applying
- Add to organization pool option

### Branding Pool Management

When setting branding for a service with an organization:

1. Select branding from full catalog
2. Preview
3. Choose whether to add to organization pool
4. Apply to service

At `/services/{service_id}/service-settings/set-{type}-branding/add-to-branding-pool-step`:
- "Yes" - Add to org pool for reuse
- "No" - Apply to this service only

## Logo File Guidelines

### Email Logos

**PNG format:**
- Transparent background preferred
- 300-600px width recommended
- RGB color mode
- 72-150 DPI
- Optimize file size

**SVG format:**
- Vector shapes only
- No embedded text (convert to paths)
- No embedded images
- No JavaScript
- Optimize/simplify paths

### Letter Logos

**SVG format (required):**
- Single color (black) or spot color
- No gradients
- No text elements (paths only)
- Suitable for black & white printing
- Simple, clean design
- High contrast

<Warning>
  Complex logos may not print well. Test by sending yourself a letter before using in production.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Logo won't upload">
    Common issues:
    - File size exceeds 2MB
    - Wrong format (must be PNG/SVG for email, SVG for letters)
    - SVG contains embedded images or text
    - File is corrupted
    
    Solutions:
    - Compress/optimize image
    - Convert format
    - Clean SVG (convert text to paths, remove embedded content)
    - Re-export from design software
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Colors don't match brand guidelines">
    Email colors must meet accessibility requirements:
    - Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1
    - Use tools like WebAIM Contrast Checker
    - Adjust colors if needed
    - Request help from Notify team
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Logo appears too large/small">
    Notify automatically sizes logos:
    - Email: Scaled to fit header area
    - Letters: Fixed height (~25mm)
    
    If sizing is off:
    - Check source file dimensions
    - Ensure adequate whitespace around logo
    - Contact support for custom sizing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="NHS/Government option not available">
    Check:
    - Service organization type is set correctly
    - Organization is NHS or central government
    - Service is linked to organization
    
    If issues persist, contact platform admin.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Branding not in organization pool">
    To add branding to pool:
    - Create new branding (automatically added)
    - Platform admin can add when setting branding
    - Contact organization admin
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Preview doesn't show logo">
    If preview fails:
    - Check browser console for errors
    - Try different browser
    - Verify file uploaded successfully
    - Clear browser cache
    - Contact support if persists
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
